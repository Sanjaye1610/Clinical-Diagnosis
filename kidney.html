Kidney Disease Prevention Tips

Why is Kidney Health Important?

Your kidneys play a critical role in filtering waste and balancing fluids in your body. Maintaining kidney health can help prevent diseases and improve your overall well-being.

Prevention Tips

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and flush toxins from your kidneys.
  • Limit your salt intake to maintain healthy blood pressure levels.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Exercise regularly to manage weight and reduce the risk of kidney-related complications.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Control diabetes and high blood pressure with regular check-ups and medication as prescribed.
  • Avoid overusing over-the-counter pain relievers, which can harm your kidneys.
  • Get your kidney function tested regularly if you have risk factors like diabetes, hypertension, or a family history of kidney disease.
