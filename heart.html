Diagnosis: Heart Disease

Based on the analysis, it has been identified that you may have heart disease. Please consult a cardiologist for further evaluation and treatment.

Prevention Tips:

  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein.
  • Engage in regular physical activity, such as walking, swimming, or cycling, for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Avoid smoking and reduce exposure to secondhand smoke.
  • Limit alcohol consumption to moderate levels.
  • Monitor and control your blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques like deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.
  • Maintain a healthy weight to reduce the strain on your heart.
